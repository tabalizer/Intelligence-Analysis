# 🔍 5W1H Framework

## 🛑 What
Classifying **what** has happened helps determine the nature of an event:

- 🔐 **Security Event**
- ⚠️ **Safety Event**
- 📌 **General Event/Incident**
- 📝 **Event Descriptions**

---

## ⏳ When
Time-related details help in tracking and correlation:

- 📅 **Date & Time**
- ⏰ **Hour, Minutes, Seconds**
- 🌍 **Time Zones:**
  - ⏳ **Zulu Time**
  - 🌎 **GMT**
  - 🇪🇺 **CET**
  - 🌍 **UTC**
